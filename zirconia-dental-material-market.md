<p><p>The Zirconia dental material market is primarily categorized into two types: Zirconia dental discs and Zirconia dental blocks. Zirconia dental discs are thin, circular shapes used in CAD/CAM systems for producing crowns and bridges, offering high strength and aesthetic appeal. In contrast, Zirconia dental blocks are solid, rectangular pieces designed for the same applications but provide versatility in milling and are suitable for a wider range of dental restorations. Both types are favored for their biocompatibility and durability.</p></p>
