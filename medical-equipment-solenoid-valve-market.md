<p><strong>The Medical Equipment Solenoid Valve Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Acting</li><li>Pilot Operated</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical equipment solenoid valve market is divided into two main types: direct acting and pilot operated valves. Direct acting solenoid valves operate directly through the electromagnetic force, allowing for quick and precise fluid control, ideal for low-flow applications. In contrast, pilot operated solenoid valves use a pilot mechanism to control a larger flow of fluid, making them suitable for high-pressure systems. Both types are essential in medical devices, ensuring efficient fluid management in medical applications.</p></p>
