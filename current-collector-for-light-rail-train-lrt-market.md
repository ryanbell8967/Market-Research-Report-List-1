<p><strong>The Current Collector for Light Rail Train (LRT) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Top-Running</li><li>Bottom-Running</li><li>Side-Running</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The current collector for Light Rail Transit (LRT) systems primarily falls into three types: top-running, bottom-running, and side-running systems. Top-running current collectors are mounted above the rail, utilizing an overhead wire to deliver power, ensuring a streamlined design. Bottom-running collectors are positioned beneath the train, using a conductor rail for power supply, minimizing visual impact. Side-running systems, less common, feature power supply from the side of the train, enhancing operational flexibility. Each type serves specific infrastructure and operational needs.</p></p>
