<p><strong>The Semiconductor Wafer Used Electrostatic Chucks (ESC) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Coulomb Type</li><li>Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor wafer electrostatic chuck (ESC) market is primarily categorized into two types: Coulomb type and Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) type. Coulomb type ESCs utilize electrostatic forces to grip the wafer, providing effective stability during processing. In contrast, JR type ESCs employ a unique principle where the electric field creates a clamping effect across a specific gap, enhancing thermal management and uniformity. Both types are crucial for semiconductor manufacturing, ensuring precise handling and optimal performance in fabrication processes.</p></p>
