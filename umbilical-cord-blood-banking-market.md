<p><p>The umbilical cord blood banking market is divided into two main types: public and private cord blood banks. Public cord blood banks collect and store donated cord blood for use by anyone in need of stem cell transplants, promoting accessibility and altruism. In contrast, private cord blood banks offer storage services for families, allowing them exclusive access to their child's stem cells for potential future medical use. Each type serves distinct purposes, catering to different consumer needs and preferences.</p></p>
