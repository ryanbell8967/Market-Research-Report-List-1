<p><p>The pipe extrusion machine line market includes single screw and twin screw extruders. Single screw extruders are cost-effective, simple, and ideal for basic polymer processing, making them suitable for standard pipe production. Twin screw extruders offer advanced mixing, higher output, and better material handling, making them preferred for complex materials and high-performance pipes. Both types are essential in the pipe manufacturing industry, with single screw extruders catering to simpler applications and twin screw extruders addressing specialized, high-demand requirements.</p></p>
