<p><strong>The Sonar Buoy Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Sonar Buoy</li><li>Passive Sonar Buoy</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sonar buoy market comprises two main types: active and passive sonar buoys. Active sonar buoys emit sound waves that bounce off underwater objects, allowing for the detection and ranging of submarines and marine life. In contrast, passive sonar buoys listen for sounds emitted by vessels or aquatic creatures without transmitting signals. Active systems are generally used for locating and tracking, while passive systems excel in surveillance and intelligence-gathering, providing complementary capabilities in underwater acoustic monitoring.</p></p>
