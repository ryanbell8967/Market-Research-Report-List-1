<p><p>The olefinic thermoplastic elastomer market comprises primarily two types: Thermoplastic Polyolefins (TPO) and Thermoplastic Vulcanizates (TPV). TPOs are blends of polyolefins and rubber, offering excellent elasticity and resistance to heat and chemicals, making them suitable for automotive and industrial applications. TPVs, on the other hand, are produced through dynamic vulcanization, resulting in materials that combine the properties of thermoplastics and vulcanized rubber, providing enhanced flexibility and durability. Both types are valued for their versatility and sustainability in various industries.</p></p>
