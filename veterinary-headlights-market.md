<p><strong>The Veterinary Headlights Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>LED</li><li>Halogen</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The veterinary headlights market primarily comprises two types: LED and halogen. LED headlights are favored for their energy efficiency, long lifespan, and bright illumination, making them ideal for detailed examinations and surgeries. They produce minimal heat and can have adjustable brightness settings. In contrast, halogen headlights offer a more traditional choice, providing a warm light that is easy on the eyes but generates more heat and has a shorter lifespan. Both types cater to the specific needs of veterinary professionals.</p></p>
