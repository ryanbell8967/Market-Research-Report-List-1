<p><p>Metal belt conveyors are categorized into three main types: fixed, adjustable, and combination. Fixed conveyors have a set length and are ideal for consistent, unchanging pathways in industrial settings. Adjustable conveyors feature extendable or retractable mechanisms, allowing flexibility for varying workspaces or product sizes. Combination conveyors integrate both fixed and adjustable elements, offering versatility to accommodate different tasks. Each type serves distinct operational needs in manufacturing and logistics, enhancing efficiency and optimizing material handling processes.</p></p>
