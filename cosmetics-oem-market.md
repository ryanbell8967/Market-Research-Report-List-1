<p><strong>The Cosmetics OEM Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>All process OEM</li><li>Half process OEM</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Cosmetics OEM market is categorized into two main types: All Process OEM and Half Process OEM. All Process OEM involves complete product development, including formulation, manufacturing, packaging, and labeling, allowing brands to outsource the entire process. In contrast, Half Process OEM provides flexibility by handling only specific stages, such as formulation or manufacturing, with brands managing other aspects like packaging and marketing. These models enable brands to streamline production based on their capabilities and needs.</p></p>
