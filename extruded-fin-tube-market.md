<p><p>The extruded fin tube market primarily consists of two types: single metal tubes and bimetallic tubes. Single metal tubes are made from a single material, usually aluminum or copper, providing efficient heat transfer and durability. Bimetallic tubes, on the other hand, combine two metals to enhance thermal conductivity and resistance to corrosion, offering improved performance in specialized applications. Both types are essential in industries like HVAC, petrochemical, and power generation, where efficient heat exchange is crucial.</p></p>
