<p><p>The alkaline proteases market is primarily categorized into two types: serine proteases and metalloproteases. Serine proteases, characterized by a serine residue in their active site, are widely used in detergent formulations and food processing due to their stability and efficacy at high pH levels. Metalloproteases, requiring metal ions for activity, play crucial roles in biotechnological applications like protein hydrolysis and waste treatment. Both types are vital in various industries, including food, pharmaceuticals, and biotechnology, driven by demand for efficient enzymatic processes.</p></p>
