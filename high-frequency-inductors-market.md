<p><ul><li>Wire Wound Type</li><li>Film Type</li><li>Multilayer Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The high-frequency inductors market is primarily categorized into three types: wire wound, film, and multilayer. Wire wound inductors utilize copper wire coiled around a core, ideal for high current applications. Film inductors consist of thin layers, providing excellent performance at high frequencies with minimal parasitic effects. Multilayer inductors, constructed with multiple stacked layers, offer compact size and high inductance values, making them suitable for modern electronic devices. Each type serves distinct applications in telecommunications, automotive, and consumer electronics.</p></p>
