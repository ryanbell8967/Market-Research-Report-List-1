<p><p>The continuous reactors market comprises various types including tubular reactors, fixed bed reactors, fluid bed reactors, and continuous stirred tank reactors. Tubular reactors feature a narrow cylindrical shape for efficient mixing and heat transfer, ideal for high-pressure reactions. Fixed bed reactors use stationary catalysts, suitable for gas-solid reactions. Fluid bed reactors suspend solid catalysts in a fluid, enhancing mass transfer. Continuous stirred tank reactors maintain constant flow and mixing, making them versatile for diverse applications in chemical processes, pharmaceuticals, and food industries.</p></p>
