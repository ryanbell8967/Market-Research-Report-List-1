<p><p>The peptides extraction kits market consists of various sorbent types crucial for isolating peptides from biological samples. C18 sorbents, known for their reversed-phase properties, effectively trap hydrophobic peptides. C8 sorbents, with shorter carbon chains, offer slightly different selectivity and can enhance recovery for specific peptide sizes. Silica sorbents provide a more polar option, suitable for diverse applications. Other sorbent types may include ion-exchange and hydrophilic interaction models, catering to specialized extraction needs based on peptide characteristics and sample matrices.</p></p>
