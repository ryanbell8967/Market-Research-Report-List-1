<p><strong>The Car Backup Camera Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CCD Cameras</li><li>CMOS Cameras</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The car backup camera market primarily features two types: CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) and CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor) cameras. CCD cameras are known for high image quality, better low-light performance, and lower noise, making them ideal for premium vehicles. CMOS cameras, on the other hand, are cost-effective, energy-efficient, and compact, suitable for mass-market cars. Both types enhance safety by providing clear rearview visuals, but CCD is preferred for superior imaging, while CMOS dominates due to affordability and integration ease.</p></p>
