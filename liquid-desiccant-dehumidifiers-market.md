<p><p>Liquid desiccant dehumidifiers utilize hygroscopic solutions to absorb moisture from the air, enhancing indoor air quality. The market is categorized into two primary types: organic solvent and inorganic solvent. Organic solvents, typically derived from carbon-based compounds, offer advantages such as lower corrosion rates and efficiency at lower temperatures. In contrast, inorganic solvents, like lithium chloride or calcium chloride, are often more cost-effective and have higher moisture absorption capacity. Both types play crucial roles in applications ranging from industrial to residential settings.</p></p>
