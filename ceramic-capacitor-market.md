<p><p>The ceramic capacitor market comprises several key types. Multilayer Ceramic Chip Capacitors (MLCCs) are compact and widely used in electronics due to their high capacitance and reliability. Ceramic Disc Capacitors, known for their simple design and durability, are often employed in high-voltage applications. Feedthrough Ceramic Capacitors are utilized in electronic enclosures, providing electrical isolation while allowing signals to pass through. Ceramic Power Capacitors are designed for high-power applications, offering excellent performance in filtering and energy storage.</p></p>
