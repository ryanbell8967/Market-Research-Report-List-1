<p><p>The microscope illuminator market primarily features two types: halogen and LED lighting. Halogen illuminators offer bright, white light and a broader spectrum but generate more heat and have a shorter lifespan. In contrast, LED illuminators are energy-efficient, have a longer operational life, and produce less heat, making them safer for delicate specimens. Both types cater to various microscopy applications, with LED technology increasingly favored for its longevity and lower energy consumption, thus driving market growth.</p></p>
