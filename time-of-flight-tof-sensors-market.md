<p><strong>The Time of Flight ToF Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>RF-Modulated Light Sources</li><li>Range Gated Imagers</li><li>Direct Time-Of-Flight Imagers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Time of Flight (ToF) sensors are critical in depth sensing applications and can be categorized into three market types: </p><p>1. **RF-Modulated Light Sources** utilize frequency-modulated light to measure distances based on phase shifts, offering high precision in various lighting conditions.</p><p>   </p><p>2. **Range Gated Imagers** employ time-gated detection to capture images at specific intervals, enhancing performance in challenging environments with variable lighting.</p><p>3. **Direct Time-Of-Flight Imagers** directly calculate distances by measuring the time taken for emitted light to return, providing rapid and accurate depth information suitable for real-time processing.</p></p>
