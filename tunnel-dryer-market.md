<p><p>The tunnel dryer market features distinct types based on drying mechanisms: Solar Type and Blower Type. Solar tunnel dryers utilize solar energy to heat air, which then passes through the product, ideal for regions with abundant sunlight. Conversely, blower type tunnel dryers employ mechanical fans to circulate heated air, providing consistent drying regardless of weather conditions. Both types are essential in agricultural processing, ensuring efficient moisture removal while preserving product quality, catering to diverse drying needs in various industries.</p></p>
