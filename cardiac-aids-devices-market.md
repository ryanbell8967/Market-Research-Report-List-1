<p><p>The cardiac aid devices market includes various types aimed at supporting heart function. Ventricular Assist Devices (VADs) help pump blood from the ventricles to the body, ideal for patients with weakened hearts. Full Artificial Hearts replace the heart altogether, offering a temporary solution for severe heart failure. Intra-aortic Balloon Pumps enhance coronary perfusion and reduce cardiac workload during surgery. Other devices encompass additional technologies and support mechanisms used to assist heart function, catering to diverse patient needs.</p></p>
