<p><strong>The Surface Management Temperature Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contact-Type Temperature Sensor</li><li>Noncontact-Type Temperature Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Surface Management Temperature Sensor market comprises two main types: contact-type and non-contact-type sensors. Contact-type temperature sensors measure temperature through direct contact with the surface, ensuring accurate readings but requiring physical interaction with the object. In contrast, non-contact-type sensors utilize infrared technology to gauge temperature from a distance, allowing for rapid measurements of moving or inaccessible surfaces. Each type serves distinct applications, with contact sensors typically used in controlled environments and non-contact sensors favored for dynamic or hazardous conditions.</p></p>
