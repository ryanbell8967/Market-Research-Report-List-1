<p><strong>The GaN Epitaxial Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CVD</li><li>MBE</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The GaN epitaxial market is primarily segmented into two types: Chemical Vapor Deposition (CVD) and Molecular Beam Epitaxy (MBE). CVD involves the deposition of thin films through chemical reactions of gaseous precursors, allowing for high-quality, uniform layers suitable for power electronics and optoelectronic applications. In contrast, MBE utilizes beams of atoms or molecules in a vacuum to create extremely precise and controlled layers, making it ideal for high-performance devices. Each method offers unique advantages for specific applications in semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
