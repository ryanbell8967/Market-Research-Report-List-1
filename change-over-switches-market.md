<p><p>Change-over switches are essential devices used to manage the switching between multiple power sources. The market is divided into two main types: automatic and manual. Automatic change-over switches detect power supply failures and switch sources without human intervention, ensuring uninterrupted power supply. In contrast, manual change-over switches require a user to manually select the desired power source, providing control but necessitating more user involvement. Each type serves different applications, from residential to industrial settings, based on reliability and user preference.</p></p>
