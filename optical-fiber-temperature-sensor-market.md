<p><strong>The Optical Fiber Temperature Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dot Type Temperature Sensor</li><li>Distributed Type Temperature Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The optical fiber temperature sensor market comprises two main types: dot type and distributed type sensors. Dot type temperature sensors focus on specific points, providing precise temperature readings at designated locations within a system. In contrast, distributed type sensors use fiber optics to measure temperature continuously along the entire length of the fiber, allowing for real-time monitoring across extensive areas or complex structures. Both types cater to various industries, ensuring efficient temperature management in diverse applications.</p></p>
