<p><p>The disposable microvascular clips market is categorized into absorbable and non-absorbable types. Absorbable clips gradually dissolve in the body, minimizing the need for surgical removal and reducing long-term complications. They are often used in delicate procedures where healing is prioritized. Non-absorbable clips, on the other hand, provide permanent fixation and are made from materials that remain in the body indefinitely. They are typically used in situations requiring strong, lasting attachment. Both types cater to different surgical needs and patient outcomes.</p></p>
