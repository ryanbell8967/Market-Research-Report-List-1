<p><p>The paintable silicone market consists primarily of two types: squeeze tubes and cartridges. Squeeze tubes offer convenience for small, quick projects, allowing for easy application and controlled dispensing. They are ideal for touch-ups or minor repairs. Cartridges, on the other hand, are designed for larger applications, providing a steady flow of silicone for filling gaps or sealing joints. Cartridges typically require a caulking gun for application, making them suitable for professional use and extensive projects.</p></p>
