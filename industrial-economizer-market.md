<p><strong>The Industrial Economizer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fluid Economizers</li><li>Air-side Economizers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial economizer market primarily comprises two types: fluid economizers and air-side economizers. Fluid economizers utilize liquid, typically water, to recover waste heat from industrial processes, improving energy efficiency and reducing operational costs. Air-side economizers, on the other hand, leverage outdoor air to optimize HVAC systems by allowing cooler outside air to reduce the need for mechanical cooling. Both types enhance energy efficiency and sustainability in industrial applications, contributing to lower energy consumption and greenhouse gas emissions.</p></p>
