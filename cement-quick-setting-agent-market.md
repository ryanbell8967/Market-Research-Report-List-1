<p><p>The cement quick setting agent market is primarily divided into two types: inorganic and organic. Conversely, organic quick setting agents often include polymers and cellulose derivatives that enhance workability and reduce setting time while providing additional benefits like flexibility. Both types cater to diverse construction needs, ensuring rapid setting under various environmental conditions, thereby increasing efficiency in construction projects.</p></p>
