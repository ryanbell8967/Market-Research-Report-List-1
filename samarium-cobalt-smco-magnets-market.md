<p><p>Samarium-Cobalt (SmCo) magnets are categorized into two main types: SmCo5 and Sm2Co17. SmCo5 magnets consist of a single-phase structure and are known for their high thermal stability, making them ideal for high-temperature applications. They typically offer lower energy density compared to Sm2Co17. Sm2Co17 magnets are a two-phase structure, providing superior magnetic properties and higher energy density, making them suitable for applications requiring strong magnetic performance in compact spaces. Both types are used in various industries, including automotive and aerospace.</p></p>
