<p><strong>The Transformer Cores Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Steel Laminated Cores</li><li>Solid Cores</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Transformer cores are essential components in electrical transformers, influencing efficiency and performance. The market is primarily divided into two types: steel laminated cores and solid cores. Steel laminated cores consist of thin sheets of steel, reducing energy loss through eddy currents, making them suitable for high-efficiency applications. In contrast, solid cores are made from a single piece of material, offering simplicity in design but with potentially higher energy losses. The choice between these types depends on application requirements and efficiency goals.</p></p>
