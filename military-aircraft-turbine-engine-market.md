<p><ul><li>Turbojet Engine</li><li>Turbofan Engine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The military aircraft turbine engine market primarily encompasses two main types: turbojet engines and turbofan engines. Turbojet engines operate by combusting fuel and expelling exhaust through a nozzle, offering high speeds but lower efficiency at subsonic speeds. In contrast, turbofan engines feature a large fan that provides additional thrust, making them more fuel-efficient and quieter, ideal for modern military jets. Both engine types are crucial for different operational requirements and tactical advantages in various military missions.</p></p>
