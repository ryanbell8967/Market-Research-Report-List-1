<p><p>The electrostatic field meter market is categorized into non-contacting and contacting types. Non-contacting electrostatic field meters measure electric fields without direct contact, allowing for safe assessments of electrostatic charge in sensitive environments. In contrast, contacting meters require direct contact with the charged surface to measure the field strength more accurately. Both types serve crucial roles in industries such as electronics, pharmaceuticals, and manufacturing, where controlling static electricity is essential to ensure product quality and safety.</p></p>
