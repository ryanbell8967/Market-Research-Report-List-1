<p><p>Amorphous metal ribbons are advanced materials characterized by a non-crystalline structure, offering unique magnetic and mechanical properties. The market is primarily divided into iron-based and cobalt-based types. Iron-based amorphous ribbons, known for their high permeability and low cost, are widely used in transformers and electrical applications. Cobalt-based ribbons, while more expensive, exhibit superior thermal stability and magnetic performance, making them suitable for specialized applications like sensors and high-frequency devices. Both types cater to growing demand in electronics and energy sectors.</p></p>
