<p><p>The Multi-Channel Remote ECG Monitor market is segmented into two main types: Wired and Wireless Multi-Channel Remote ECG Monitors. Wired Multi-Channel monitors utilize cables to connect to ECG devices, providing stable, high-quality signals and often requiring less maintenance. In contrast, Wireless Multi-Channel Remote ECG Monitors use Bluetooth or Wi-Fi technology for data transmission, offering increased mobility and ease of use for both patients and healthcare providers. Both types enhance patient monitoring and facilitate timely diagnosis of cardiac conditions.</p></p>
