<p><p>The regenerated catalyst market comprises two primary types: off-site regeneration and on-site regeneration. Off-site regeneration involves transporting spent catalysts to specialized facilities for processing, allowing for efficient recovery and recycling of valuable materials. In contrast, on-site regeneration occurs directly at the production facility, enabling immediate reuse and minimizing logistical challenges. Both methods aim to enhance sustainability in industrial processes, reduce waste, and lower operational costs, but they vary in terms of implementation, convenience, and resource management.</p></p>
