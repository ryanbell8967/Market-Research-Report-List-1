<p><strong>The Current Sensing Chips Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open-loop Current Sensor</li><li>Closed loop Current Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The current sensing chips market is segmented into open-loop and closed-loop current sensors. Open-loop current sensors measure the magnetic field generated by the current, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness, making them ideal for low-precision applications. Closed-loop current sensors, on the other hand, utilize feedback mechanisms to provide high accuracy and linearity, suitable for applications requiring precise current measurements. Both types cater to various industries, including automotive, industrial automation, and renewable energy, driven by increasing demand for energy efficiency and system monitoring.</p></p>
