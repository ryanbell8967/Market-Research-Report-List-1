<p><p>The thermal imaging infrared camera market is classified into three main types based on wavelength. Short-wave infrared cameras capture wavelengths from 0.9 to 2.5 microns, ideal for applications like industrial inspections and security. Mid-wave infrared cameras operate between 3 to 5 microns and are commonly used in military and surveillance applications for detecting thermal signatures. Long-wave infrared cameras, which range from 8 to 14 microns, excel in building inspections and HVAC applications, effectively detecting heat loss and moisture issues.</p></p>
