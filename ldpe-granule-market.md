<p><strong>The LDPE Granule Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Autoclave Process</li><li>Tubular Process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The LDPE granule market encompasses two main production processes: the autoclave process and the tubular process. </p><p>The autoclave process involves polymerizing ethylene in a high-pressure reactor, delivering high molecular weight polyethylene with excellent ductility and clarity, suitable for applications like film and packaging. In contrast, the tubular process employs a continuous reactor, enabling faster production and cost efficiency. This method yields lower-density polyethylene ideal for robust applications such as containers and flexible packaging, allowing manufacturers to meet diverse market demands effectively.</p></p>
