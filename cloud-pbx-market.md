WAN refers to cloud-based telephony solutions catering to geographically dispersed organizations, facilitating communication across multiple locations via the internet. In contrast, the LAN market focuses on local telephony solutions within a confined area, like an office building, enabling seamless connectivity among nearby devices. Both types leverage cloud infrastructure but serve different organizational scopes and networking requirements.</p></p>
