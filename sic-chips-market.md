<p><strong>The SiC Chips Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SiC Trench Gate Chip</li><li>SiC Planar Gate Chip</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The SiC chips market primarily consists of two types: SiC Trench Gate Chips and SiC Planar Gate Chips. SiC Trench Gate Chips utilize a three-dimensional structure that allows for higher efficiency and lower on-resistance, making them suitable for high-power applications. In contrast, SiC Planar Gate Chips feature a traditional flat design, providing simpler manufacturing processes but typically lower performance. Both types cater to sectors like automotive, renewable energy, and industrial machinery, driving advancements in power electronics.</p></p>
