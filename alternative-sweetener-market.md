<p><ul><li>Low-intensity Sweetener (LIS)</li><li>High-intensity Sweetener (HIS)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Alternative Sweetener Market includes Low-intensity Sweeteners (LIS) and High-intensity Sweeteners (HIS). LIS, such as polyols, provide mild sweetness with fewer calories than sugar, often used in reduced-calorie products. HIS, like stevia and aspartame, are intensely sweet, requiring minimal quantities to match sugar's sweetness, making them calorie-free or low-calorie options. Both cater to health-conscious consumers seeking sugar alternatives, with LIS preferred for bulk and texture, while HIS is ideal for calorie reduction and intense sweetness in beverages and processed foods.</p></p>
