<p><strong>The Ceramic Filtering Membrane Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>The ceramic filtering membrane market is categorized into three primary types: microfiltration, ultrafiltration, and nanofiltration. Microfiltration utilizes membranes with larger pore sizes to separate particles and bacteria, commonly used in water treatment. Ultrafiltration is designed for smaller molecules, effectively removing dissolved solids and colloids, often employed in food and beverage industries. Nanofiltration, with even smaller pore sizes, selectively separates divalent ions and small molecules, making it ideal for water softening and certain industrial applications. Each type addresses specific filtration needs, enhancing efficiency and quality across various sectors.</p></p>
