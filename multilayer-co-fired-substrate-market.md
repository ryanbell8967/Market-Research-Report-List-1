<p><p>The multilayer co-fired substrate market comprises two main types: Low-Temperature Co-fired Ceramics (LTCC) and High-Temperature Co-fired Ceramics (HTCC). LTCC substrates are used for devices requiring flexible designs and are ideal for high-frequency applications, featuring lower processing temperatures. In contrast, HTCC substrates are designed for high-performance requirements, withstanding elevated temperatures and harsh environments, making them suitable for aerospace and automotive applications. Both types are essential for advanced electronic packaging solutions, optimizing performance and reliability.</p></p>
