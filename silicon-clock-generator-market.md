<p><ul><li>Surface Mount</li><li>Through Hole</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The silicon clock generator market can be segmented into surface mount and through-hole types. Surface mount clock generators are designed for compact, high-density applications, easily integrated onto printed circuit boards with automated assembly processes. They offer advantages like reduced space and enhanced performance. Conversely, through-hole clock generators are typically used in applications requiring more robust connections and are mounted using holes in the PCB. These may be preferred in environments where durability and stability are critical, such as in aerospace or industrial settings.</p></p>
