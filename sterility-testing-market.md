<p><p>The sterility testing market encompasses various methods, primarily direct inoculation and membrane filtration. Direct inoculation involves introducing a sample directly into a culture medium, allowing for the detection of microbial growth. In contrast, membrane filtration uses a filter to capture microorganisms from the sample, which are then cultured for growth assessment. Additionally, the "others" category includes alternative methodologies such as rapid sterility testing and automated systems, catering to diverse industry needs and enhancing testing efficiency and accuracy.</p></p>
