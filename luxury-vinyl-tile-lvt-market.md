<p><p>Luxury Vinyl Tile (LVT) market encompasses various types tailored for different installation needs and performance attributes. Stone Plastic Composite (SPC) features a rigid core for enhanced strength and water resistance, while Wood Plastic Composite (WPC) combines comfort with acoustic benefits. Other market segments include various specialty LVT products designed for specific applications or aesthetics.</p></p>
