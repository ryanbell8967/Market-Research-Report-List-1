<p><p>The Automotive Active Bonnet is a safety feature designed to reduce pedestrian injuries in the event of a collision. It works by deploying sensors that detect an impact with a pedestrian, causing the bonnet to automatically lift and create a cushioning effect. This mechanism helps absorb the impact force, minimizing head and upper body injuries. The system is increasingly being adopted in modern vehicles as part of advanced safety technologies.</p><p>The Automotive Active Bonnet Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.1% during the forecast period.
