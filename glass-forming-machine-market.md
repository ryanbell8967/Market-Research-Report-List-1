<p><p>The glass forming machine market is categorized into three main types: Blow & Blow, Press & Blow, and Narrow Neck Press and Blow (NNPB). </p><p>Blow & Blow involves blowing air into a molten glass parison to shape it into containers, suitable for items like bottles. Press & Blow combines pressing and blowing for more complex shapes, ideal for thicker glass products. NNPB specifically uses a narrow neck design, enhancing efficiency for producing bottles and jars with a precise finish, particularly for narrow containers.</p></p>
