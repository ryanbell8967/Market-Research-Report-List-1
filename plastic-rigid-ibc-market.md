<p><p>The plastic rigid Intermediate Bulk Container (IBC) market consists of three main types: HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene) Rigid IBC, LLDPE (Linear Low-Density Polyethylene) Rigid IBC, and LDPE (Low-Density Polyethylene) Rigid IBC. HDPE IBCs offer superior chemical resistance and durability, making them suitable for a wide range of industrial applications. LLDPE IBCs provide flexibility and impact resistance, ideal for transporting sensitive materials. LDPE IBCs, while less common, offer excellent barrier properties, making them suitable for specific applications requiring low permeation.</p></p>
