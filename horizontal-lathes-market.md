<p><ul><li>Horizontal Guide Rail Lathes</li><li>Inclined Rail Lathes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The horizontal lathe market is divided into two main types: horizontal guide rail lathes and inclined rail lathes. Horizontal guide rail lathes feature a flat bed design that maintains workpiece stability, ideal for precision machining of various materials. In contrast, inclined rail lathes have a tilted bed, allowing for better chip removal and improved cutting tool accessibility. Both types cater to distinct machining needs, offering versatility and efficiency in industrial manufacturing settings, particularly in metalworking and fabrication environments.</p></p>
