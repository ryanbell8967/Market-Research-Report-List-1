<p><strong>The Arteriosclerosis Testers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PWV</li><li>CF-PWV</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Arteriosclerosis testers include various non-invasive devices that measure arterial stiffness, crucial for assessing cardiovascular health. The two primary types are Pulse Wave Velocity (PWV) and Central Blood Pressure Wave Velocity (CF-PWV). PWV assesses the speed at which pressure waves travel through arteries, indicating stiffness levels, while CF-PWV provides insights into central arterial stiffness specifically, offering a more targeted evaluation of cardiovascular risk. Both methods facilitate early detection and management of arteriosclerosis, promoting preventive healthcare strategies.</p></p>
