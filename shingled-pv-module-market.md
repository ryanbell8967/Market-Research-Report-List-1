<p><p>The shingled PV module market consists of two main types: single-sided modules and dual-sided modules. Single-sided modules feature overlapping cells that enhance efficiency and reduce shading losses, making them ideal for residential and commercial applications. In contrast, dual-sided modules capture sunlight from both sides, allowing for increased energy generation in suitable environments. This feature is particularly advantageous in places where reflected light can be harnessed, such as snowy or sandy regions. Both types aim to maximize energy output and efficiency in solar applications.</p></p>
