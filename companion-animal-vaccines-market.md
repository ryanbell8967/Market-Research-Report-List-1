<p><p>The companion animal vaccines market encompasses various types designed to enhance pet health. Attenuated live vaccines contain weakened pathogens, stimulating a strong immune response. Inactivated vaccines use killed microbes to induce immunity, while subunit vaccines employ specific antigen fragments. Conjugate vaccines link bacterial polysaccharides to proteins for better response, and toxoid vaccines neutralize toxins from pathogens. DNA vaccines introduce genetic material to provoke an immune response, while recombinant vaccines use genetically engineered organisms to produce antigens, offering targeted protection.</p></p>
