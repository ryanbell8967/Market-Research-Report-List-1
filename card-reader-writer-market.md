<p><p>The card reader-writer market can be divided into two primary types: wireless and wired. Wireless card readers utilize technologies such as Bluetooth or NFC to facilitate contactless transactions, offering portability and convenience for mobile payment systems. In contrast, wired card readers connect directly to devices via USB or other interfaces, ensuring stable and secure data transfer. Each type serves distinct user preferences and operational requirements, with wireless options favoring flexibility and mobility, while wired solutions emphasize reliability and robustness in transactions.</p></p>
