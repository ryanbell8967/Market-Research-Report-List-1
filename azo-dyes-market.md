<p><strong>The Azo Dyes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Disperse dyes</li><li>Metal-complex dyes</li><li>Reactive dyes</li><li>Substantive dyes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Azo dyes market includes various types: **Disperse dyes** are water-insoluble, used for synthetic fibers like polyester. **Metal-complex dyes** form stable complexes with metals, offering excellent light and wash fastness for wool and nylon. **Reactive dyes** chemically bond with fibers, ideal for cotton and cellulose, ensuring vibrant, durable colors. **Substantive dyes** directly adhere to fibers without mordants, commonly used for cotton and viscose. Each type caters to specific textile applications, balancing colorfastness, fiber compatibility, and environmental considerations.</p></p>
