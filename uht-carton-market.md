<p><p>UHT cartons, or Ultra-High Temperature cartons, are packaging solutions used for preserving liquid food products such as milk, fruit juices, and soups. The UHT carton market is witnessing significant growth driven by the rising consumer demand for convenient and long-lasting food products. </p><p>Market growth is fueled by increasing urbanization, a busy lifestyle that encourages on-the-go consumption, and rising awareness of food safety and hygiene. Innovations in packaging technology, including eco-friendly materials and smart packaging features, are further propelling market trends. </p><p>Distribution channels are diversifying, with e-commerce and online grocery shopping playing a pivotal role in market expansion.
