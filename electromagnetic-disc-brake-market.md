<p><p>The electromagnetic disc brake market features two primary types: single disc and multi-disc systems. Single disc brakes utilize one disc for braking, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness, commonly found in motorcycles and light vehicles. In contrast, multi-disc brakes incorporate multiple discs stacked together, providing enhanced braking performance and heat dissipation, making them suitable for high-performance cars and heavier applications. Both types leverage electromagnetic actuation for efficient braking, offering distinct advantages based on performance requirements and vehicle design.</p></p>
