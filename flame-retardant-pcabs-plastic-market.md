<p><p>Flame retardant PC/ABS plastic is primarily used in two molding processes: injection molding and extrusion molding. Injection molding involves heating the plastic and injecting it into a mold, allowing for intricate designs and high production efficiency, making it ideal for components like electronics housings. Extrusion molding, on the other hand, forces melted plastic through a die to create continuous shapes like sheets or tubes, suited for applications such as automotive parts and construction materials. Both methods ensure enhanced safety and performance in various applications.</p></p>
