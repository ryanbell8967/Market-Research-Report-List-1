<p><p>The insulating electrical gloves market is categorized into three types based on voltage ratings: low voltage, medium voltage, and high voltage. Low voltage insulating gloves provide protection against voltages up to 1,000 volts and are commonly used in general electrical work. Medium voltage gloves are designed for voltages ranging from 1,000 to 15,000 volts, suitable for utility and industrial applications. High voltage insulating gloves offer protection for voltages over 15,000 volts, typically used by electrical linemen and in high-risk environments to ensure safety during maintenance and operation.</p></p>
