<p><p>The biomass steam boiler market encompasses various systems, notably Combined Heat and Power (CHP) systems and cogeneration technologies. CHP systems simultaneously generate electricity and useful thermal energy from biomass, enhancing energy efficiency. These systems utilize biomass, such as wood chips or agricultural residues, as fuel, providing a sustainable energy solution. Cogeneration, a subset of CHP, focuses specifically on optimizing the heat and power output, maximizing overall efficiency, reducing greenhouse gas emissions, and promoting renewable energy usage in industrial and commercial applications.</p></p>
