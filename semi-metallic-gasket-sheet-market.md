In contrast, non-planar flange gaskets are engineered for irregular or uneven surfaces, ensuring a tight seal even in complex geometries. Both types cater to various industries, including oil and gas, automotive, and manufacturing, offering enhanced durability and resistance to extreme temperatures and pressures for critical sealing applications.</p></p>
