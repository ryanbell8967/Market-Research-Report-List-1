<p><p>The protein expression systems market is diverse, featuring multiple platforms for producing recombinant proteins. Mammalian protein expression systems are preferred for post-translational modifications, offering high fidelity for complex proteins. Baculovirus systems utilize insect cells for efficient protein production and high yields. Prokaryotic systems, such as E. coli, are favored for rapid production and cost-effectiveness, but may lack post-translational modifications. Yeast protein expression systems balance productivity and modification capabilities, making them versatile for various biotechnological applications.</p></p>
