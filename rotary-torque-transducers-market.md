<p><p>The rotary torque transducer market is categorized into two main types: dynamic and static. Dynamic torque transducers measure torque in real-time during motion, making them ideal for applications like motors and engines where constant torque assessment is critical. Static torque transducers, on the other hand, gauge torque when the system is stationary, suitable for testing and calibration purposes. Both types are crucial in various industries, including automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing, ensuring precise torque measurement for performance and safety.</p></p>
