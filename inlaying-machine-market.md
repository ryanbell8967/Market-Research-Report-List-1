<p><strong>The Inlaying Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hot-pressing Inlaying</li><li>Cold-pressing Inlaying</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The inlaying machine market consists of two primary types: hot-pressing inlaying and cold-pressing inlaying. Hot-pressing inlaying utilizes heat and pressure to bond materials, making it suitable for applications requiring strong adhesion and durability. In contrast, cold-pressing inlaying employs adhesive without heat, allowing for a more delicate approach and often used in sensitive materials. Both methods cater to diverse industries, including woodworking, automotive, and electronics, providing specific advantages based on material properties and production needs.</p></p>
