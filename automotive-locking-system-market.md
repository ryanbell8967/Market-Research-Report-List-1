<p><strong>The Automotive Locking System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Powered Automatic Locking System</li><li>Manual Locking System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive locking system market comprises two main types: powered automatic locking systems and manual locking systems. Powered automatic locking systems enhance vehicle security by locking doors automatically when the vehicle is in motion or when the key fob is out of range. These systems often integrate advanced features like remote access and smartphone connectivity. In contrast, manual locking systems require physical intervention from the user to secure the vehicle, typically through a key or lever, offering a simpler and more cost-effective solution.</p></p>
