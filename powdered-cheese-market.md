<p><p>The powdered cheese market is categorized into two main types: pure cheese powder and cheese powder blends. Pure cheese powder is made from dehydrated, single-variety cheese, offering concentrated flavor and nutritional value. It is commonly used in snacks, sauces, and seasonings. Cheese powder blends, on the other hand, combine different cheese types or incorporate additional ingredients to achieve unique flavors and functionalities, often utilized in processed foods, seasonings, and culinary applications, enhancing versatility in food products.</p></p>
