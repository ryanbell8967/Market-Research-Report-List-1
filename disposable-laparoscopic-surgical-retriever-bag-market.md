<p><p>The disposable laparoscopic surgical retriever bag market is categorized into two types: detachable and non-detachable. Detachable retriever bags are designed to separate from their delivery devices after use, allowing for easier removal and minimizing the risk of contamination. Non-detachable bags, on the other hand, remain attached to the delivery device during extraction, providing a secure method for handling specimens. Both types serve the purpose of safely collecting and containing tissue during minimally invasive surgeries, enhancing procedural efficiency and safety.</p></p>
