<p><p>The lubricating paste market encompasses various types, with two prominent categories being BN (Boron Nitride) and MoS2 (Molybdenum Disulfide). BN lubricating paste provides excellent thermal and chemical stability, making it ideal for high-performance applications and extreme conditions. In contrast, MoS2 offers outstanding anti-friction properties and reduces wear in machinery, enhancing operational efficiency. Both types cater to diverse industries, including automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing, where performance and durability are critical to prolonging machinery lifespan and ensuring smooth operations.</p></p>
