<p><p>The solar cell paste market primarily consists of three types: Aluminum (Al) paste, Silver (Ag) front paste, and Silver (Ag) back paste. Al paste is used for creating the rear contact on silicon solar cells, enhancing their efficiency and durability. Ag front paste facilitates the formation of the front electrodes, crucial for light absorption and current collection. Ag back paste serves as a reflective layer, improving energy conversion. Each type plays a vital role in optimizing solar cell performance.</p></p>
