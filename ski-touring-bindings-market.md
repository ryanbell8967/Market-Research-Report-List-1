<p><strong>The Ski Touring Bindings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Frame Bindings</li><li>Tech Bindings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ski touring bindings are categorized into two main types: frame bindings and tech bindings. Frame bindings resemble traditional alpine bindings, offering a robust ramp for downhill performance while allowing uphill motion. They are ideal for beginners or those prioritizing stability. In contrast, tech bindings, also known as pin bindings, are lightweight and designed for efficiency, featuring a minimalist design with pins that secure the boot. They provide superior climbing capabilities, making them preferred by advanced skiers seeking mobility and performance in varied terrains.</p></p>
