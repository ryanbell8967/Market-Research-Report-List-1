<p><p>Flat Panel Displays (FPD) are widely used technologies for TVs, monitors, and screens, characterized by their slim design. The Organic Light Emitting Diode (OLED) market offers vibrant colors and deep blacks due to self-emissive pixels. Liquid Crystal Display (LCD) technology relies on backlighting and liquid crystals to create images, making it energy-efficient but sometimes less dynamic in color. Plasma Display Panels (PDP) use ionized gases to produce images, excelling in color accuracy and larger sizes, though they consume more power.</p></p>
