<p><p>Clear coatings are categorized into several market types based on their formulation and application methods. Solvent-borne coatings use organic solvents for application and provide a smooth finish, often used in industrial and automotive sectors. Water-borne coatings utilize water as a primary solvent, offering lower VOC emissions and easier cleanup, making them popular for environmentally conscious applications. Powder coatings are applied as a dry powder and cured under heat, ensuring durability and resistance. Other market types may include UV-cured and radiation-cured options, each catering to specific needs.</p></p>
