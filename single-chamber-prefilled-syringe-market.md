<p><p>The single chamber prefilled syringe market is primarily segmented into glass and plastic types. Glass syringes are favored for their inert properties, ensuring drug stability and longer shelf life, particularly for sensitive biologics. They provide excellent barrier protection but can be heavier and more fragile. Meanwhile, plastic syringes, often made from materials like cyclo-olefin copolymer (COC), offer lighter weight, flexibility, and reduced risk of breakage. They are increasingly popular due to their cost-effectiveness and ease of manufacturing.</p></p>
