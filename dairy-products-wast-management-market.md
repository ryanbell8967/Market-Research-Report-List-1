<p><p>Dairy products waste management encompasses various treatment methods aimed at reducing environmental impact. Aerobic digestion, or composting, involves microbial decomposition in the presence of oxygen, transforming waste into nutrient-rich compost. Anaerobic digestion processes waste in oxygen-free environments, producing biogas for energy and digestate as a fertilizer. Incineration or combustion involves burning waste to generate heat and energy while reducing its volume significantly. Other methods may include biochemical treatments, landfill, or innovative technologies, each targeting efficiency and sustainability in waste handling.</p></p>
