<p><strong>The Agriculture Heat exchanger Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Plate</li><li>Tubular</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The agriculture heat exchanger market is primarily divided into two types: plate and tubular heat exchangers. Plate heat exchangers consist of many thin, corrugated plates stacked together, providing high thermal efficiency and compact size, making them ideal for applications requiring quick heat transfer. Conversely, tubular heat exchangers utilize a series of tubes to transfer heat between fluids, offering robustness and ease of maintenance. Both types serve crucial roles in agricultural processes, enhancing energy efficiency and productivity in operations like heating, cooling, and pasteurization.</p></p>
