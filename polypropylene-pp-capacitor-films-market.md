<p><p>The polypropylene (PP) capacitor films market is primarily divided into two types: smooth PP film and rough PP film. Smooth PP films are characterized by their uniform surface and are typically used in high-frequency applications due to their low dielectric loss and excellent insulation properties. In contrast, rough PP films feature a textured surface, enhancing their adherence to electrodes and improving capacitance in power electronics. Both types cater to specific applications in various industries, including automotive, consumer electronics, and renewable energy.</p></p>
