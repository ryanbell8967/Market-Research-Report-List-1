<p><strong>The Vehicle Horn Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Air Horn</li><li>Electronic Horn</li><li>Electromagnetic Horns</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vehicle horn market is primarily categorized into three types: air horns, electronic horns, and electromagnetic horns. Air horns use compressed air to produce a loud sound, often found in trucks and buses for warning. Electronic horns generate sound through electrical signals, offering a variety of tones and are common in passenger vehicles. Electromagnetic horns function by vibrating a diaphragm with electrical current, providing a traditional and reliable option. Each type serves specific applications and preferences in automotive sound signaling.</p></p>
