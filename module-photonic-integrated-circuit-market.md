<p><strong>The Module Photonic Integrated Circuit Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive Photonic Integrated Circuit</li><li>Active Photonic Integrated Circuit</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The photonic integrated circuit market is categorized into two main types: passive and active. Passive photonic integrated circuits primarily manipulate light without requiring external power, utilizing components like waveguides and filters to direct and control optical signals. In contrast, active photonic integrated circuits contain elements that require power to function, such as lasers and modulators, enabling dynamic control of light for applications in telecommunications and data processing. Both types are essential for advancing photonic technology in various industries.</p></p>
